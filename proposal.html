IV. Our Investment Process
1
Discovery & Financial PlanningA comprehensive discovery process and risk assessment establishes goals, timeline, income needs, and capacity for risk. Your financial plan, maintained in eMoney, is developed as a separate engagement before any portfolio decision is made and is reviewed annually.
2
Portfolio ConstructionYour portfolio is built from the QWM Model Portfolios — a disciplined allocation matched to your risk profile. Holdings are selected based on a range of criteria, including cost-effectiveness, liquidity, transparency, and portfolio role. No commissions. No product sales. No conflicts.
3
Ongoing MonitoringPortfolio drift is monitored systematically via iRebal. Performance is reported quarterly through Black Diamond. The financial plan is maintained in eMoney and reviewed annually or upon any material life change.
4
Tactical Risk ManagementIn confirmed negative market environments, Quarters Wealth Management, LLC applies a documented protocol: quality/value tilts within the equity sleeve, defensive fixed income adjustments, and incremental deployment of buffered ETFs — in an effort to mitigate downside impact without abandoning long-term allocation targets.
5
Regular Ongoing ReviewsFormal reviews are conducted annually — or sooner upon any material life change — covering portfolio performance, financial plan progress, goal alignment, and any needed adjustments to strategy or allocation. Quarterly performance reports are delivered through Black Diamond. You are encouraged to reach out at any time between scheduled reviews.
V. Why Quarters Wealth Management, LLC

Most advisory relationships are built around a portfolio. At Quarters Wealth Management, LLC, the portfolio is built around a plan — and that difference shapes everything we do.

Before any investment decision is made, we develop a comprehensive financial plan that maps your goals, timeline, income needs, and capacity for risk. The portfolio follows from that plan. Every holding, every allocation, and every rebalance is deliberate and tied directly to where you are going — not just where markets are. Our investment process is documented, our model portfolios are disciplined, and we review your portfolio on an ongoing and as-needed basis so that decisions are never reactive.

Our fee is straightforward (please see the fee agreement) and is based on assets under management. We earn nothing from products, transactions, or referrals. We primarily use low-cost, transparent ETFs — and where appropriate, select active strategies — because cost and quality discipline compound meaningfully over time. And because we work with a focused number of clients, you have direct access to your advisor — not a call center, not a junior associate — when it matters most.
